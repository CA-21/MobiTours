Democratic Republic of Congo

Located in the heart of Central Africa, the Democratic Republic of Congo is also called Congo - Kinshasa (the name of its capital) to distinguish it from its neighbor, the Republic of Congo, also known as Congo - Brazzaville. With over 2.3 million square kilometers, Democratic Republic of Congo is the second largest country in Africa by area, and has a population of about 68 million people. The northern part of R.D.C. is one of the largest areas of rain forest in the world. The game, which borders the East African Rift, is the area of ??mountains, hills, lakes and volcanoes.
